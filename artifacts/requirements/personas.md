## Personas

### Persona Name: Administrator
- Description: A privileged user who governs access, team structure, and system-level task controls.
- Business Goals: Maintain secure access, keep task workflows consistent, and oversee team operations.
- Responsibilities: Manage users, teams, roles, task visibility, reports, and account state.
- Pain Points: Inconsistent access, unmanaged task sprawl, and lack of clear accountability.
- Permissions: Full control over users, teams, tasks, reporting, and administration settings.
- Primary Screens: Dashboard, Task List, Settings, User Management, Reports
- Primary Business Processes: User administration, team governance, reporting, and task oversight

### Persona Name: Team Lead
- Description: A supervisor responsible for coordinating team work and monitoring progress.
- Business Goals: Keep work aligned to deadlines and ensure team visibility.
- Responsibilities: Create tasks, assign work, monitor progress, and review team-focused reports.
- Pain Points: Workload imbalance, missed deadlines, and unclear task status.
- Permissions: Create and manage tasks for assigned teams, view reports, and manage team membership within their scope.
- Primary Screens: Dashboard, Task List, Task Details, Reports
- Primary Business Processes: Team workload monitoring, assignment, review, and progress tracking

### Persona Name: Team Member
- Description: A contributor who performs assigned work and collaborates on tasks.
- Business Goals: Complete assigned work clearly, communicate progress, and stay organized.
- Responsibilities: Manage owned tasks, update progress, comment, attach work, and manage personal profile preferences.
- Pain Points: Lost visibility, unclear ownership, and delayed communication.
- Permissions: View assigned tasks, update owned tasks, comment, manage personal profile and preferences.
- Primary Screens: Task List, Task Details, Profile, Settings
- Primary Business Processes: Task execution, status updates, collaboration, and personal organization
