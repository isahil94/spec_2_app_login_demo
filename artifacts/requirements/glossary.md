## Business Terms
| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Task | A work item that must be created, assigned, tracked, and completed. |
| Assignee | The user currently responsible for completing a task. |
| Reporter | The user who created or initiated a task. |
| Owner | The user accountable for a task’s business ownership. |
| Team | A group of users who collaborate on shared work. |
| Team Lead | A role responsible for coordinating a team’s work and assignments. |
| Archived Task | A task that remains visible but is read-only until restored. |
| Notification | A user-facing alert for assignment, status, comment, due-date, or overdue activity. |
| Dashboard | The main landing view summarizing task and team activity. |
| Profile | A user’s personal account information and preferences. |

## Domain Definitions
- Status: The current lifecycle state of a task, such as Todo, In Progress, Review, Completed, or Blocked.
- Priority: The relative urgency or importance of a task.
- Audit History: A record of significant task and account changes for accountability.

## Key Concepts
- Role-Based Access: Access that depends on the user’s business role and permissions.
- Task Lifecycle: The progression of a task through business-defined states from creation to completion or archive.
