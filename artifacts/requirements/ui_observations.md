## Navigation and Workflow Expectations
- Users shall move from authentication into an overview dashboard and then into task-focused flows.
- Task management shall support create, review, update, archive, and restore actions from list and detail views.
- Profile and settings shall be reachable from account-related navigation.

## Business UI Expectations
- The interface shall present clear task status, priority, due date, ownership, and activity context.
- Empty, success, validation, and error states shall be explicit and user-friendly.
- Navigation and form controls shall support keyboard use and accessible labeling.

## Accessibility and Responsive Expectations
- The experience shall preserve clear visual hierarchy and contrast across screen sizes.
- Interactive elements shall be operable without a mouse and remain understandable to assistive technology users.

## Design Alignment Notes
- The visual presentation should closely follow the supplied Figma reference.
- Any justified deviation from the Figma design must remain aligned to business usability and accessibility expectations.
