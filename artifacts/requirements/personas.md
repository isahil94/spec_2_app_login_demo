## Personas

### Persona Name: Administrator
- Description: A person responsible for platform governance, user administration, and broad operational oversight.
- Business Goals: Maintain secure access, control users and teams, and ensure task processes remain consistent.
- Responsibilities: Manage users, teams, roles, settings, reports, and task recovery actions.
- Pain Points: Too much manual oversight, inconsistent permissions, and delayed visibility into team work.
- Permissions: Full access to administrative, reporting, and recovery functions.
- Primary Screens: Dashboard, User Management, Team Management, Reports, Settings.
- Primary Business Processes: User lifecycle management, team governance, reporting, and system configuration.

### Persona Name: Team Lead
- Description: A role focused on coordinating team work and guiding assigned tasks to completion.
- Business Goals: Keep projects moving, assign work clearly, and monitor team progress.
- Responsibilities: Create and assign tasks, manage team members, review progress, and oversee team performance.
- Pain Points: Missed handoffs, unclear ownership, and difficulty tracking due dates.
- Permissions: Manage tasks and team membership within assigned teams, with restricted global administration.
- Primary Screens: Dashboard, Task List, Task Details, Team Management, Reports.
- Primary Business Processes: Planning, assignment, monitoring, and team-level follow-up.

### Persona Name: Team Member
- Description: A contributor who manages assigned work and participates in day-to-day task execution.
- Business Goals: Complete assigned work efficiently and stay informed about task context.
- Responsibilities: Create personal tasks, update owned tasks, comment, upload attachments, and monitor progress.
- Pain Points: Scattered task context, missed notifications, and limited visibility into shared work.
- Permissions: Manage personal tasks and profile settings, with limited administrative access.
- Primary Screens: Dashboard, Task List, Task Details, Profile, Settings.
- Primary Business Processes: Task execution, progress updating, collaboration, and personal configuration.

## Notes
- The personas remain business-focused and implementation-agnostic.
- They align to the role, permission, and workflow expectations in the specification.
